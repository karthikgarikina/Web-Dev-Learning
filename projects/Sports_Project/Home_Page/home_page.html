Legacy of Excellence

Aditya has proudly secured fourteen consecutive Intercollegiate Athletics Championships, a testament to its relentless commitment to sporting excellence alongside academic distinction. The college deeply believes that sports and extracurricular activities are vital pillars of holistic education, integrating them into the academic framework to build confidence, leadership, and team spirit among students. This culture encourages active participation and nationwide recognition of student talent year after year.

World-Class Facilities

To support its legacy, Aditya provides top-tier sports infrastructure for students to train and excel. The campus boasts a full-fledged Athletic Track, Basketball Court, Football and Cricket Fields, Practice Nets, and Kabaddi, Handball, Throw Ball, and Tennikoit Courts, among others. These well-maintained facilities foster daily practice and ensure every athlete has the tools to thrive. Students have access to an energetic and competitive atmosphere right on campus.
